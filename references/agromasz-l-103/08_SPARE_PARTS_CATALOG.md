## How to Order Spare Parts

Spare parts can be obtained from the manufacturer or from the machine's retailer. When ordering spare parts, please provide the following data:

- Catalogue designation of the part (Katalogbezeichnung),
- Catalogue number (Katalognummer),
- Quantity (Stückzahl),
- Machine symbol, year of manufacture, factory/serial number (data from the type plate / Typenschild),
- Exact address of the ordering party.

> **NOTE:** The "data from the type plate" identify the exact loader build — always copy machine symbol, build year (Baujahr) and factory number (Fabriknummer) from the type plate when ordering.
