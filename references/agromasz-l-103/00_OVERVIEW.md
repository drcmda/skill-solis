# Agromasz Ł-103U — Front Loader (300 kg)

> **Source:** Agromasz Ł-103U Agricultural Front Loader (300 kg) — Operating Manual & Spare-Parts Catalogue (*Bedienungsanleitung / Ersatzteilkatalog*), **Zakład Metalowy "AGROMASZ"**, Mrągowo, Poland — publication 01/202/6/2020 (Oct 2020). **Translated from the German edition** into English; original German terms are kept in parentheses on first use. 18 chapters + spare-parts catalogue → 8 references.

This is the reference hub for the **Agromasz Ł-103U front loader** (*Frontlader*) — a tractor-mounted front-end loader with a quick-attach tool carrier. It is the **loader itself**: it bolts to the **front of the tractor** (on a subframe/console) and runs off the **tractor's hydraulics**. Work tools (bucket, forks, grab, blade) mount on its tool carrier.

> ✅ **Approved for the Solis 26.** The manual explicitly lists **Solis 26, Solis 26 HST, Solis 26 9+9** (and Farmtrac B26 / B30) as the tractors this loader is designed for — so this loader and the tractor in this skill are a matched pair.

> 🔗 The **[Quicke Multigrab C/CS grab bucket](../quicke-multigrab-c/00_OVERVIEW.md)** is exactly the kind of work tool that mounts on a front loader like this (on its tool carrier / quick-attach frame).

## What it is

- A **tractor front-end loader** (*Frontlader*), nominal lift **300 kg**, with two lift and two tilt cylinders and a quick-attach tool carrier.
- Mounts on the **tractor's front** via a bolted subframe/console (*Anbaukonsole*); driven by the **tractor's hydraulics** (direct, or via an additional sectional valve type II/III).
- Operated from the cab by a **control valve / joystick (Steuergerät)** — raise/lower the boom, crowd/dump the tool, with a **float position (Schwimmstellung)**.
- Requires a rear **counterweight (Gegengewicht)** for stability.

## Work tools (attachments)

Bulk bucket, manure fork, pallet fork, **"Crocodile" silage/manure grab**, front dozer blade, and a 4-in-1 levelling bucket. They mount on the loader's tool carrier / quick-attach frame — see [03 — Assembly & Attachment Change](03_INSTALLATION_AND_CONNECTION.md).

## Quick safety essentials

- **Only approved tractors** (above); a **rear counterweight is mandatory**; never operate without it.
- **Never carry or lift people**; never stand or work **under a raised boom**.
- Drive with the **boom low (~0.5 m)** and the load tucked; max **working speed 6 km/h**, **transport 15 km/h**; slope limits **12° up/down, 8° across**.
- **Road travel only with the work tool removed**, mechanical cylinder locks fitted, and the SMV triangle displayed.
- Do **not** use the front loader and a **front three-point linkage** at the same time; do not operate the control valve from off the tractor seat.
- The hydraulic **accumulator/shock absorber is nitrogen-charged** — do not open; **replace hydraulic hoses every 4 years**.
