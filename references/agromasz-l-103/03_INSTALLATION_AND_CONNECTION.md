# 03 — Assembly, Tractor Connection & Attachment Change

> **Source:** Agromasz Ł-103U Agricultural Front Loader (300 kg) — Operating Manual & Spare-Parts Catalogue (Bedienungsanleitung / Ersatzteilkatalog), Zakład Metalowy "AGROMASZ", Mrągowo, Poland — German edition, translated to English — pp.16–19.

This chapter covers the physical assembly of the front loader (Frontlader) onto the tractor: fitting the supporting subframe/console to the tractor, coupling the loader boom to the consoles with its hydraulics and locking pins, and changing the working tool on the quick-attach frame. The subframe is fitted once (first commissioning) by a qualified technician; coupling/uncoupling the loader and swapping attachments is then a routine operator task.

## Construction Overview (§5)

The front loader (Frontlader) (Fig. 2) consists of the following main components:

| Item | Component (German) | English |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Ausleger | Boom / lift arm (the loader's main lifting structure) |
| 2 | Stützkonstruktion | Supporting structure / subframe console (mounts to the tractor) |
| 3 | Schaufel (Arbeitswerkzeug) | Bucket (working tool / attachment) |
| 4 | Auflagestützen | Parking / rest supports (stands the boom takes when detached) |
| 5 | Gegengewicht auf dem Gewichtsträger | Counterweight on the weight carrier |

The loader's hydraulic system (Hydraulikanlage) can be connected **directly** to the tractor's hydraulic system, **or** via an additional section-divider/valve block (Abschnittsverteiler) of type II or III.

> **NOTE:** Fig. 2 ("Frontlader mit Traktor" / Front loader on the tractor) shows the loader mounted on the tractor with items 1–5 labelled as above.

### Compatible Attachments (Working Tools)

The loader can be equipped with the following working tools (Arbeitswerkzeuge):

- Bucket for bulk material (Schaufel für Schüttgut)
- Manure fork (Mistgabel)
- Pallet fork (Palettengabel)
- Silage or manure grab, "Crocodile" type (Silage- oder Mistgreifer Typ "Krokodil") — listed twice in the German source ("Silage- oder Mistgreifer Typ 'Krokodil', Silage- oder Mistgreifer Typ 'Krokodil'"), apparently to denote both a silage grab and a manure grab variant
- Front dozer blade / leveling blade (Frontplanierraupe)
- 4-in-1 leveling bucket (4 in 1 Schaufelnivellierung) — a combination of toothed bucket, scraping blade, and grab

## §6 — Assembly (Montage)

### 6.1 General Provisions (Allgemeine Bestimmungen)

- The manufacturer ships the loader boom (Laderausleger) **fully assembled**.
- The supporting structure (Tragkonstruktion / subframe), the required attachments, and the fastening elements are shipped **disassembled**.
- The small parts to be mounted on the tractor and the hydraulic components are packed **inside the counterweight box (Gegengewichtskasten)**.
- The loader is supplied with the accessories previously agreed with the buyer.

### 6.2 Mounting the Front Loader (Subframe to Tractor)

> **IMPORTANT NOTICE:** Assembly/disassembly of the supporting structure (Tragkonstruktion) and the hydraulic system on the tractor may only be carried out by a qualified service technician of the manufacturer or dealer. **The user is not permitted to perform this assembly/disassembly themselves.**

This task consists of mounting the elements of the supporting structure (Tragkonstruktion) onto the tractor. The structural elements are fastened **only at first commissioning**; thereafter the tractor remains adapted for working with a front loader.

**Procedure:**

1. **Remove the front-axle weights** (Gewichte der Vorderachse) from the tractor before mounting the supporting structure.
2. **Bolt on the subframe (Unterkonstruktion):** the left and right console/support (linke und rechte Stütze), the mounting plate (Befestigungsplatte), and the tie rods/struts (Stangen).
   - Ensure you use the **correct bolts** for the threaded holes (Gewindebohrungen) in the tractor.
   - Use **class 8.8 bolts** to fasten the structure.
   - Tighten the bolted joints to the torques in the table below.
3. **Mount the hydraulic distributor/valve block (Hydraulikverteiler)** on the tractor and connect it to the tractor's external hydraulic system (externes Hydrauliksystem).
   - The hydraulic distributor is mounted on the **right-hand side of the tractor cab**, after removing the lower window pane (untere Scheibe).
4. **Fit a weight carrier (Gewichtsträger) with a counterweight (Gegengewicht) at the rear of the tractor.**

> **NOTE:** Fig. 3 ("Zugmaschine mit aufgehängtem Gegengewicht" / Tractor with suspended counterweight) shows the tractor fitted with the rear weight carrier and counterweight.

### Bolt Tightening Torques (Class 8.8)

Tighten the bolted connections to the following torques:

| Thread size (Maß) | Torque (Drehmoment), Nm |
| --- | --- |
| M6 | 10 |
| M8 | 25 |
| M10 | 49 |
| M12 | 85 |
| M14 | 135 |
| M16 | 210 |
| M18 | 300 |
| M20 | 425 |
| M24 | 730 |

## §7 — Connecting the Front Loader to the Tractor

### Coupling Procedure

To connect the loader to the tractor (Zugmaschine), proceed as follows:

1. Drive the tractor — with the supporting structure 2 (Fig. 2) already mounted — **up to the boom 1**, which is parked resting on the rest supports 4 (Auflagestützen).
2. **Lower the remaining rest supports 4** so that the boom comes to rest on the **quick-couplings (Schnellkupplungen)** of the consoles.
3. **Secure the quick-couplings with the cotter pins / split pins (Splinte).**
4. **Remove (dismount) the rest supports (Auflagestützen).**
5. **Connect the loader's hydraulic lines** to the tractor's external hydraulic circuit (externer Hydraulikkreislauf) **or** to a pre-mounted distributor/valve block.
   - The individual sockets and plugs of the quick-couplings are **marked with numbers** for correct connection — match the numbers.
6. **Fit a weight carrier (Gewichtsträger) with a counterweight at the rear of the tractor.**
7. The tractor must be **equipped with a set of rear-axle weights**, and the **front-axle weights must be removed (demounted).**

> **NOTE:** Fig. 4 ("Frontlader in der Ruheposition" / Front loader in the rest/parked position) shows the boom standing on its rest supports, ready to be coupled.

### Ballasting / Rear-Axle Loading

It is **categorically required** that **no less than 20 % of the total weight** (tractor + loader + tool + counterweight + load) be loaded onto the **rear axle** of the tractor. At least 20 % of the load on the rear axle is required to enable driving and braking during operation of the tractor/front-loader combination.

Additional loading of the rear wheels (cast-iron weights, pumping water into the tyres) must be done in accordance with the operating instructions applicable to each tractor type.

> **IMPORTANT NOTICE:** If you have difficulty determining the optimal load capacity, contact the manufacturer of the loader, who will advise you accordingly.

> **IMPORTANT NOTICE:** Working with the loader requires **increasing the pressure by 25 %** relative to the catalogue pressure in the **front tyres** of the tractor.

> **IMPORTANT NOTICE:** It is **forbidden** to adapt the loader to other tractors by yourself / on your own (i.e. without the manufacturer or dealer). [lit.: "Es ist verboten, den Lader allein auf andere Traktoren zu adaptieren."]

### Connection & Coupling Safety Notices

> ⚠️ **IMPORTANT NOTICE:** When coupling and uncoupling the loader to/from the tractor, **particular caution is required.** Switch off the tractor engine, remove the ignition key, and apply the parking brake (Handbremse).

> ⚠️ **IMPORTANT NOTICE:** Use **standard cotter/split pins (typische Splinte)** to secure the pins. It is **forbidden** to use other securing devices (e.g. bolts, rods, wires), as this can damage the loader and the tractor and endanger the operator.

## §8 — Mounting and Dismounting the Attachment

To exchange the working unit (Arbeitseinheit) of the loader, proceed as follows (see Fig. 5):

1. **Lower the boom (Ausleger)** until the working unit 2 rests on the ground.
2. **Unlock the locking pins (Verriegelungsstifte)** by moving the lever 4 (Verriegelungshebel).
3. **Rotate the frame 3 downward** by using the tractor, then **back the tractor away (reverse out).**
4. **Insert the new working unit** into the guides of frame 3 and **lock it with the pins** by moving the lever 4.

> **NOTE:** Fig. 5 ("Ankuppeln der Schaufel an den Schnellwechselrahmen" / Coupling the bucket to the quick-attach frame) labels the parts as below.

| Item | Component (German) | English |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Ausleger | Boom / lift arm |
| 2 | Arbeitseinheit | Working unit / attachment (e.g. bucket) |
| 3 | Schnellwechselrahmen | Quick-attach frame / tool carrier |
| 4 | Verriegelungshebel | Locking lever |

> ⚠️ **IMPORTANT NOTICE:** **Particular caution is required when changing the working unit** of the loader. Switch off the tractor engine, remove the ignition key, and apply the parking brake (Handbremse).
