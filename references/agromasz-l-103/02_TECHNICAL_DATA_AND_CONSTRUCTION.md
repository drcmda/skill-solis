| 2 | Symbol / model code (Symbol) | Ł-103 (T202/6) |

| 15 | Cylinder type — frame (Zylindertyp (Rahmen)) | 40/25/250 |

**Cylinder dimension key.** Cylinder type codes are given as **bore / rod diameter / stroke** in mm:
- **Lift cylinders (Hubzylinder) — "Heben":** 40/25/440 → 40 mm bore, 25 mm piston-rod diameter, 440 mm stroke.
- **Tilt / frame cylinders (Kippzylinder) — "Rahmen":** 40/25/250 → 40 mm bore, 25 mm piston-rod diameter, 250 mm stroke.

**Pressure note.** Maximum operating oil pressure is **16 MPa = 160 bar**. The loader runs off the tractor's (Traktor) hydraulic system; ensure the tractor hydraulics can deliver this pressure.

**Counterweight note.** The loader weight figure of **300 kg** breaks down as **120 + 180 kg** (the two main weight components), and is quoted **without bucket and without counterweight (Gegengewicht)**. The bulk-material bucket adds **65 kg**.

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** Speed-limit decal on the rear of the counterweight (Gegengewicht) restricts travel on public roads to **15 km/h**. Do not exceed the transport speed of 15 km/h; working speed is 6 km/h. (See §3 / safety decals.)

## §5 Construction / Structure (Aufbau)

The front loader (Frontlader), shown in **Fig. 2 (Abb. 2 — "Front loader with tractor" / Frontlader mit Traktor)**, consists of the following main assemblies:

| No. (Pos.) | Component (German term) | Description |
|------------|-------------------------|-------------|
| 1 | Boom / loader arm (Ausleger) | The lifting arm assembly that raises and lowers the work tool; delivered fully assembled by the manufacturer. |
| 2 | Support structure / mounting subframe (Stützkonstruktion) | The carrying/support frame that mounts the loader to the tractor's front (the subframe/console). Delivered disassembled. |
| 3 | Bucket / work tool (Schaufel / Arbeitswerkzeug) | The attached implement — standard is the bulk-material bucket; interchangeable with other tools (see attachments list). |
| 4 | Parking / support stands (Auflagestützen) | The stands the loader rests on when detached/parked from the tractor. |
| 5 | Counterweight on the weight carrier (Gegengewicht auf dem Gewichtsträger) | Ballast counterweight mounted on its carrier/rack to balance raised front loads. |

> **NOTE:** Fig. 2 caption (Abb. 2): "Front loader with tractor." Legend — 1 = boom/arm (Ausleger), 2 = support structure/subframe (Stützkonstruktion), 3 = bucket/work tool (Schaufel / Arbeitswerkzeug), 4 = parking/support stands (Auflagestützen), 5 = counterweight on the weight carrier (Gegengewicht auf dem Gewichtsträger).

The diagram depicts the assembled loader fitted to the front of a tractor: the support structure (2) is bolted to the tractor; the boom/arm (1) pivots up from it carrying the bucket/work tool (3) at the front; the counterweight (5) sits at the rear on the tractor's hitch/weight carrier to balance the load; and the parking stands (4) are shown at the boom for detached parking.

## Available Attachments / Work Tools

The loader can be equipped with the following interchangeable work tools (in place of part 3, the standard bucket):

| Attachment (German term) | English |
|--------------------------|---------|
| Schaufel für Schüttgut | Bulk-material bucket |
| Mistgabel | Manure fork |
| Palettengabel | Pallet fork |
| Silage- oder Mistgreifer Typ "Krokodil" | Silage or manure grab ("Crocodile"-type grapple) |
| Frontplanierraupe | Front dozer blade / leveling blade |
| 4-in-1 Schaufelnivellierung | 4-in-1 leveling bucket (combination of toothed bucket, scraper blade and grab) |

> **NOTE:** The 4-in-1 bucket (4 in 1 Schaufelnivellierung) is described as a combination of a toothed bucket, a scraper/grading blade (Schürfscheibe) and a grab (Greifer). The loader is supplied with the accessories previously agreed with the buyer.

## Hydraulic Supply Options

The loader's hydraulic system (Hydraulikanlage) can be connected to the tractor's hydraulics in either of two ways:

- **Directly** to the tractor's (Traktor) hydraulic system, **or**
- Via an **additional sectional valve / directional control valve (zusätzlicher Abschnittsverteiler)** of type **II** or **III**.

> **IMPORTANT NOTICE:** Operating pressure must not exceed the **16 MPa (160 bar)** rating of the loader hydraulics (§4, Pos. 13).

## §6.1 Assembly — General Provisions (opening)

(Opening of §6 Montage as it appears on p.16; full assembly procedure continues in the next section.)

The manufacturer delivers the **loader boom/arm (Laderausleger) fully assembled**, while the **support structure (Tragkonstruktion)**, the required attachments (Anbaugeräte) and the fastening elements (Befestigungselemente) are delivered **disassembled**. The small parts to be fitted to the tractor and the hydraulic components are located **inside the counterweight box (Gegengewichtskasten)**. The loader is supplied with the accessories previously agreed with the buyer.

> **IMPORTANT NOTICE (WICHTIGER HINWEIS!):** Assembly/disassembly (Montage/Demontage) of the support structure and the hydraulic system on the tractor may **only be carried out by a qualified service technician** of the manufacturer or dealer. The **user is not permitted** to perform the assembly/disassembly.
