## 9. Control Valve (Steuerungsgerät)

The loader's hydraulic system can be connected either **directly to the tractor's (Traktor / Zugmaschine) own hydraulic system**, or by means of an additional **two- or three-section spool valve (Abschnittsverteiler / Verteiler) II or III**.

### Connection options

| Option | When it applies | What to connect |
|--------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **A** | The tractor has an **external two-circuit hydraulic system** AND the user does **not** use attachments requiring a third circuit (e.g. a bale grab / Ballengreifer). | The loader's hydraulic system is connected directly to the tractor's hydraulic system. |
| **B** | The tractor has **no** external two-circuit hydraulic system, **or** the user does not intend to use it. | A **two- or three-section valve (Verteiler)** must be connected to the external hydraulic system. The **two-section valve** controls boom (Ausleger) and bucket (Schaufel) movement. The **three-section valve** adds a working element that needs an additional supply, e.g. a bale grab (Ballengreifer) or a "crocodile"-type silage grab (Silagegreifer "Krokodil"). |
| **C** | Comfort/electro-hydraulic upgrade. | A **two-section valve plus a solenoid (electro-) valve (Elektroventil)** controlled by a push-button joystick — see [option C below](#electro-hydraulic-joystick-control-option-c). |

### Mounting the valve in the cab

The hydraulic valve (Verteiler) is mounted on the **right-hand side of the tractor cab**, after the lower window pane (untere Scheibe) has been removed.

### Hydraulic connection sequence

When connecting the hydraulic lines of the individual circuits to the valve, pay attention to the **correct positioning of the couplings (Kupplungen)**.

To make the correct connection order easier, the quick-connector sockets (Schnellverbinder) on the valve and the lines are marked with numbers:

| Valve type | Connection numbering |
|------------|----------------------|
| Two-section valve (zweiteiliger Verteiler) | **1–4** |
| Three-section valve (dreiteiliger Verteiler) | **1–6** |

> **NOTE:** Diagrams showing the principle of lever control (Hebelsteuerung) are printed on the valves themselves. The meaning of these schematics is given in Fig. 6 and Fig. 7 below.

## Lever-movement diagrams

The lever diagrams on the valve use small pictograms of a hand on a lever, with arrows showing the direction the lever is pushed/pulled and the resulting loader action. Each spool (numbered 1, 2, 3) is a separate lever / joystick axis.

### Fig. 6 — Two-section valve (zweiteiliger Verteiler)

**Loader control diagram using a two-section valve.**

| Spool (lever) | Lever movement | Loader action |
|---------------|----------------|---------------|
| **1** | Move lever one way / the other | **Lower and raise the bucket (boom)** — *Herablassen und Anheben der Schaufel (des Auslegers)* |
| **2** | Move lever one way / the other | **Dump and fill (crowd) the bucket** — *Entleeren und Abfüllen der Schaufel* |

### Fig. 7 — Three-section valve (dreiteiliger Verteiler)

**Loader control diagram using a three-section valve.**

| Spool (lever) | Lever movement | Loader action |
|---------------|----------------|---------------|
| **1** | Move lever one way / the other | **Lower and raise the bucket (boom)** — *Herablassen und Anheben der Schaufel (des Auslegers)* |
| **2** | Move lever one way / the other | **Dump and fill (crowd) the bucket** — *Entleeren und Abfüllen der Schaufel* |
| **3** | Move lever one way / the other | **Close and open the bucket/grab** — *Schließen und Öffnen der Schaufel* (third circuit, e.g. bale or silage grab) |

> **NOTE:** Spools 1 and 2 are identical in both the two- and three-section valves; the three-section valve simply adds spool **3** for the additional (third-circuit) working element.

## Electro-hydraulic joystick control (option C)

> **C: Control of the solenoid valve (Steuerung des Elektroventils)**

To improve front-loader comfort, an **electro-hydraulic control system** can be used. In this case the control element is the **push-button joystick (Druckknopf-Joystick)**. Using this system requires an efficient (functioning) electrical installation in the tractor.

The loader's hydraulic system is fitted with a **two-section valve (2-teiliger Verteiler)** and a **solenoid valve (Elektroventil)**.

- The joystick is connected to the hydraulic valve via **cables (Bowden cables / Bowdenzüge)** and to the solenoid valve via an **electrical cable**.
- The **valve** controls operation of the boom (Ausleger) and the filling and emptying (crowd/dump) of the bucket (Schaufel).
- The **solenoid valve** controls **closing and opening of the grab (Greifer)**.
- The **grab is opened or closed by pressing a button on the joystick.**

The construction and the method of control are shown in Fig. 8–11.

| Figure | Subject | What it shows |
|--------|---------|---------------|

| **Fig. 9** | Connection of Bowden cables to the control unit (*Anschluss von Bowdenzügen an das Steuergerät*) | The two Bowden cables routed from the joystick base down to the control-unit/valve body. |

**Fig. 10 — labelled callouts (electrical control circuit):**

| German label | English |
|--------------|---------|
| Taste | Button (push-button on the joystick) |
| Schieber | Slider / spool |
| Elektroventil | Solenoid (electro-) valve |
| Stecker für Traktor-Installation | Plug for the tractor's (electrical) installation |
| Stecker | Plug / connector |

The circuit runs: **joystick button (Taste) → electrical cable → plug into the tractor installation (Stecker für Traktor-Installation) → solenoid valve (Elektroventil)**, with the joystick slider (Schieber) mechanically driving the Bowden cables to the hydraulic spools and the button electrically energising the solenoid valve to open/close the grab (Greifer).
