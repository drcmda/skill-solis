## 1. Introduction (Einleitung)

- You receive this operating manual together with your front loader. At the point of sale it is the user's responsibility to read the manual carefully.
- Incorrect operation can lead to an accident or to failure of the loader, and can also reduce its efficiency and void warranty and guarantee rights.
- The operating manual forms a complete set with the machine and should accompany it throughout its entire service life. Keep it in a safe place.
- If you have any doubts or questions about the loader or the manual, contact your dealer or the manufacturer.
- If the front loader is sold or handed over to another user, please include the operating manual with it.

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** The warning symbol ⚠ (Warnsymbol) points to important information about hazards stated in the manual. When you see this symbol, protect yourself from the hazard, read the relevant information carefully, and inform other machine operators about it.

Careful reading and observance of the operating manual guarantees long and reliable operation of the machine.

## 2. Intended Use (Verwendungszweck)

The Ł-103U [300 kg] (T202/6) front loader is intended for:

- Loading and unloading agricultural crops, bulk goods and volumetric materials from heaps;
- Transporting goods stacked on pallets;
- Pushing and sorting soil or other loose materials.

> **CAUTION:** Using the bucket on hard, rough surfaces — e.g. concrete or asphalt — is unsuitable and causes excessive, accelerated wear of the cutting edge / blade (Pflugschar [lit.: ploughshare]).

After fitting a special bucket, the loader can be used for loading and unloading manure (Mist).

**Foreseeable misuse:**

- Using the loader for purposes other than those described above is regarded as misuse.
- The loader may only be used, operated and repaired by persons familiar with its construction, operation and safety practices.
- Accident-prevention regulations and all occupational safety and health regulations must always be observed.
- Any unauthorised changes to the loader made without the manufacturer's consent may release the manufacturer from liability for resulting damage or disadvantage.
- Meeting the manufacturer's requirements for use, operation and repair, with strict observance, is a condition of intended use.

**Approved tractors:** The Ł-103U [300 kg] (T202/6) is suitable for use with the following tractors (selected models): **Solis 26, Solis 26 HST, Solis 26 9+9, Farmtrac B26, or Farmtrac B30.**

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** Because of the different tyre types used on loader tractors — and especially their load capacity, the tractor weight and how that weight is distributed — the manufacturer (seller), after fitting the loader, carries out tests of unit stability and of the effect of the maximum load on movement during operation and when driving on public roads. In an unfavourable situation it is recommended, e.g., to ballast the rear axle by filling the tyres with water, or to remove weights from the front of the tractor.

> 🛑 **DANGER:** It is **not permitted** to fit the loader to, or work it on, any tractors other than those listed above.

## 3. Operating Safety (Betriebssicherheit)

> 🛑 **DANGER:** It is not permitted to operate the loader under the influence of alcohol or drugs.

### General Requirements

- In addition to the information in this manual, observe the general rules of occupational safety and health protection.
- Observe the warning symbols on the loader. Following them serves your safety.
- Observe the safety instructions in the operating manual of the tractor the loader is mounted on.
- The loader may only be operated by trained persons who are authorised to drive the tractor. Children must be excluded from the work and handling.
- Before starting work, check the condition and fastening of the individual working elements. It is forbidden to operate a damaged or disassembled machine.
- The loader's hydraulic system must also be checked. Cylinders, hydraulic hoses and connections must be leak-tight.
- Worn or damaged parts must be replaced immediately.
- The function of all hydraulic-system components must also be checked by attempting to operate the control-valve / distributor levers (Steuerhebel des Verteilers).

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** Wear protective clothing and gloves during work and operation.

### Hydraulics & Pins

> 🛑 **DANGER:** It is forbidden to work with a loader when the tractor hydraulic system is defective.

- Because of natural material wear, hydraulic hoses should be replaced periodically every **4 years** from the date of manufacture. The manufacture date is given on the hydraulic hoses: year and week number.

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** During loading, the hydraulic hoses and devices are under high pressure. When coupling and uncoupling the loader's hydraulic system to/from the tractor's, there must be **no pressure** in the lines (quick couplers / Schnellkupplungen).

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** To secure the pins, use the special spring pins (Federstifte). It is forbidden to use substitute fasteners such as bolts, wires, nails, etc., which can be cut through or fall out and thereby cause damage to the tractor and loader or even an accident.

- When repairing the loader, observe the occupational health and safety regulations for agriculture per the Regulation of the Minister of Agriculture and Food of 12 January 1998 (Polish Journal of Laws No. 12/98).

> 🛑 **DANGER:** No one may stand between the tractor and the machine until the combination is secured against rolling away — by applying the tractor parking brake or by placing chocks under the running wheels. Lower the loader gripper/boom (Greifer / Ausleger) onto the ground.

> 🛑 **DANGER:** All operating, repair and maintenance work may only be carried out with the gripper/boom (Greifer / Ausleger) lowered onto the ground and the tractor engine switched off. **Remove the key from the ignition.**

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** Operating the loader with the gripper/boom raised can cause crushing injuries.

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** Mounting/dismounting of the carrier frame, extensions and hydraulic system on the tractor may only be carried out by qualified service personnel of the manufacturer or dealer. The user is not permitted to perform mounting/dismounting.

- The loader may only be mounted on tractors recommended by the manufacturer.
- The loader can only be operated from the tractor seat position.

> 🛑 **DANGER:** It is forbidden to operate the control valve (Steuerventil) from any position other than the tractor workstation.

### Working, Loads & Stability

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** The maximum operating speed while working the loader must not exceed **6 km/h**. When working on uneven ground, reduce speed to a safe level.

> 🛑 **DANGER:** Do not carry out work with the loader on slopes steeper than **12° along the slope** and **8° across the slope**, as there is a danger that the tractor together with the front loader will tip over. Note that the tractor track width has a large influence on the stability of the combination, so on sloping ground you should work at maximum track width.

- Do not leave the loader on hillsides or other sloping ground without securing it against rolling/overturning on its own.

> 🛑 **DANGER:** The maximum load capacity must not be exceeded.

- It is forbidden to drive with the working attachment raised to maximum. All travel — including travel during work — should be carried out with the attachment lowered, so that the attachment and the transported material do not obstruct vision and, at the same time, do not scrape on the ground.
- Note that moving with a raised load increases the risk of the unit tipping over.
- Also bear in mind that the risk of the load falling onto the tractor's post/frame (Ständer) increases, especially when the loader is used to lift pallets above cab height.
- Be especially careful while loading.

> 🛑 **DANGER:** It is forbidden to stand under the raised boom (hochgezogener Ausleger), because of the danger of being crushed by structural elements or transported material.

> 🛑 **DANGER:** It is forbidden for persons to be in the working area of the loader. Make sure no bystanders are nearby before operating the loader. Pay particular attention to children.

> 🛑 **DANGER:** It is forbidden to carry persons on loaders or weight carriers, as this can lead to permanent disability or even death.

- Store the tractor–loader combination only on a horizontal, level and hardened surface, with the boom lowered to the bottom position.
- Store a removed/detached boom only on horizontal, level and hard ground. During storage the machine must be supported by two supports and a working tool.

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** Be especially careful when removing and installing the boom. Switch off the tractor engine, remove the ignition key, apply the handbrake.

- During operation, the rear of the tractor must be ballasted — with a counterweight mounted on the loader, and the rear axle loaded with weights. The front-axle weights must be removed.
- A rear-axle load is required for driving and braking the tractor/front-loader combination.

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** It is forbidden to use the loader without a counterweight.

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** Be especially careful when working near high-voltage lines. Touching the cables can cause an electric shock.

- The bucket must not be driven "out of the swing" (aus dem Schwung — i.e. with running momentum) into the material being loaded during operation, as this can damage the loader or the tractor.
- Do not scrape (level) the ground with the bucket set vertically downward.
- When working with the loader, the working path should be as short as possible.

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** When working with a load over the cab, special caution is required, as the load may fall onto the driver's cab.

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** During work, the user should pay particular attention that the loader-arm load sits exactly in the centre of the attachment. Loading the attachment from one side can cause deformation of the loading boom.

- Beware of overloading the hydraulic system through heavy tools or driving on uneven ground.
- Do not leave the loader with the boom raised. The boom must be supported on the ground when the tractor engine is switched off.
- It is not permitted to operate the bucket on uneven ground, as this leads to accelerated wear.

### Road Travel

> 🛑 **DANGER:** It is forbidden to transport straw or hay bales on public roads.

- When driving on public roads, be especially careful and comply with the applicable traffic regulations.
- Road travel may only take place **after the working attachment has been removed**. The boom must be raised so that the pivot point of the quick-attach frame (Schnellwechselrahmen / Schnellspannrahmen) is about **0.5 m** high. **Mechanical locks must be fitted on the cylinders that raise the boom.** Fit a triangular distinguishing sign (slow-moving-vehicle marking) at the rear of the tractor.

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** Driving speed is to be limited to **15 km/h**.

### Loader vs. Front Three-Point Linkage

> 🛑 **DANGER:** For safety reasons and because of possible collisions, it is forbidden to operate the tractor with both the front-loader arm and the front three-point linkage (Frontkraftheber) mounted. The loader can only be operated after the three-point linkage arms (Dreipunktgestängearme) have been removed, while the three-point linkage can only be operated after the loader's boom arms (Auslegerarme) have been removed.

### Hydraulic Shock Absorber / Accumulator

- Because of the lifting capacity, the height and for smooth lifting, a hydraulic shock absorber (hydraulischer Stoßdämpfer — accessory) is recommended for the front loader.
- A diaphragm accumulator (Membranspeicher) acts as the hydraulic shock absorber. The accumulator is filled with nitrogen at the appropriate pressure (depending on loader size — see manufacturer's data).
- The hydraulic shock absorber should be used when driving on public roads and under difficult conditions, since it protects the user, the tractor and the loader from shock loads.
- Hydraulic shock absorbers can be single- or double-acting depending on the customer order. A spare-parts kit for the hydraulic damper is shown in **Table T14**.
- The float function (Schwimmfunktion) of the hydraulic damper is active when the shut-off valve (Absperrventil) is opened.

> **IMPORTANT NOTICE:** Do not open the pressure vessel. The vessel contains nitrogen (risk of asphyxiation). The accumulator may only be filled with nitrogen, and this may only be carried out by an authorised specialist service.

> 🛑 **DANGER:** Because of the high pressure, repair of the device by the user is not permitted.

### Warning Decals on the Front Loader (Abb. 1)

Warning decals (Warnschilder) are applied to the loader to remind the user of the hazards and risks that can occur during operation. The user must pay particular attention to the points marked with warning symbols.

> **NOTE:** The operator must ensure the warning decals remain legible for the entire service life. If damaged or destroyed, they must be replaced with new ones. During loader operation, the noise emitted to the environment is caused exclusively by the tractor. Warning decals are available from the machine manufacturer; they can be sent by post on credit.

**Abb. 1 — Warning decals on the front loader (Bedeutung der Warnschilder):** The decal sheet is a grid of ISO-style yellow triangular warning pictograms plus prohibition/instruction symbols. Each numbered decal and its location:

| No. | Pictogram (described) | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Open book / read-manual symbol | Read and understand the operating manual before using the machine. *(Placed on the **left post side**.)* |
| 2 | Engine + ignition key, with prohibition/stop | Switch off the engine and remove the ignition key before beginning maintenance or repair work. *(On the **left side of the boom**.)* |
| 3 | Person falling from a raised bucket | Risk of falling from the loading bucket. Keep clear during work and transport — maintain distance from the loader (**left side of the post**). |
| 4 | Person struck/crushed by the swinging boom | Crush hazard from the loader boom. Keep your distance during work and transport (**left side of the post**). |
| 5 | Person under a raised attachment / load | Do not stand under the raised bucket or attachment (**left side of the boom**). |
| 6 | High-pressure fluid jet at hand/finger | Avoid contact with escaping pressurised hydraulic fluid — risk of losing a finger (vermeiden Sie den Einfluss von unter Druck ausströmenden Flüssigkeiten). Read the operating manual regarding maintenance work. *(On the **left side of the boom**.)* |
| 7 | Person at pinch/crush point of structure | Crush hazard (Quetschgefahr). Be especially careful, keep your distance during work and transport (**left post side of the tractor**). |
| 8 | Hand/person at hot surface | Keep a safe distance from the hot surface (in the area near the tractor's fire extinguisher). |
| 9 | Person near a rolling round bale | Keep a safe distance from a rolling bale before entering the hazard zone. |

| 12 | Person near power line | Keep a safe distance from power/electrical lines (Stromleitungen). |
