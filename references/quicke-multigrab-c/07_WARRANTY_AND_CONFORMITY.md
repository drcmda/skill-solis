This chapter sets out the manufacturer's warranty terms for the Multigrab C/CS implement and reproduces the two Declarations of Conformity that accompany the attachment: the **EU Declaration of Conformity** (p.22, per Machinery Directive 2006/42/EC Annex 2A) and the **UK Declaration of Conformity** (p.23, for the UK market). ## 7.1 Warranty Conditions

JOST Umeå AB undertakes, for a period of **12 (twelve) months** from the day that the loader/equipment was delivered to the purchaser, to replace or repair components which need to be rectified due to faults in the material or manufacturing. This is on condition that:

- the fault is **immediately notified to the supplier**, and
- the faulty loader/component is **made freely available to the supplier**.

Further terms:

- For **replaced components**, the warranty is valid only for the **remaining duration of the original warranty**.
- The warranty does **not** cover faults caused by **accidents, inadequate maintenance, modification, or incorrect installation** by the purchaser.
- When **heavily worn components** are replaced under warranty, the purchaser shall be charged for the time that they have been used.
- **No compensation** will be given for personal injury, stoppage, consequential damage, or other losses.
- **Testing or fault diagnosis** at the request of the purchaser is carried out **without charge** if defects are established in that connection. Otherwise, the purchaser is charged for **all costs**.
- **Temporary repairs** or **extra costs** incurred because work has been done **outside ordinary working hours** will **not** be compensated.

> **NOTE:** The warranty period runs from delivery of the loader/equipment to the purchaser. For warranty claims, notify the supplier immediately and refer to the loader's / base machine's user manual for the supplier contact and claim handling procedure.
