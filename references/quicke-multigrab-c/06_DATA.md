# 06 — Data

> **Source:** Quicke Multigrab C / CS Grab Bucket — Instruction Manual, Ålö AB (Quicke), Umeå Sweden — document 60081299 E, 2025-01 (English section of the multilingual manual) — Chapter 06, pp.19–21.

This chapter gives the full physical specifications of the three Multigrab models (CS 120, CM 140, CL 170) together with dimension callouts, weights, volumes, cylinder data and maximum working pressure, followed by the loader-model and lifting-capacity recommendation matrices for using the implement as a grip and as a bucket.
