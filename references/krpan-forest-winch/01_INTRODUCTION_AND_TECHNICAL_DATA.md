## The Most Important Instructions (NEJDŮLEŽITĚJŠÍ INSTRUKCE)

> 🛑 **DANGER — when working with the forest winch:** The four instructions below are reproduced from the manual's highlighted "MOST IMPORTANT INSTRUCTIONS FOR USING THE FOREST WINCH" page and must be observed with maximum care.

**1. Winding the wire rope onto the drum.**
> 🛑 **DANGER:** **WHEN YOU BEGIN WORKING WITH THE FOREST WINCH, THE STEEL/WIRE ROPE (drátěné lano / ocelové lano) MUST BE FULLY UNWOUND AND THEN FIRMLY WOUND BACK ONTO THE WINDING DRUM (naviják buben / buben).** Take care that **AT LEAST THREE wraps of rope remain on the drum**, because this prevents the rope from being pulled out of its anchoring point. When winding the rope firmly onto the drum, **assist by pulling a load**. Always handle the rope so that **NO LOOPS** form during unwinding and winding, and **NEVER GREASE / LUBRICATE THE ROPE!**

**2. Gall drive chains (Gallovy řetězy / hnací řetězy).**
> ⚠️ **WARNING:** **THE GALL DRIVE CHAINS MUST BE KEPT TENSIONED**, because they stretch slightly under load and must not run with slack. Users who operate the drive chains in heavy-duty service are advised to **lubricate them at least once a week, or at least every 50 working hours.** Lubrication must be carried out with a **special chain grease gun (mazací pistole na řetězy)**, because oil or other sprays can run onto the **clutch lining (obložení spojky)** and render the winch inoperative. Lubrication must be done **with the drive running (PTO engaged), through the grease plugs (mazací zátky)** on the winch.

**3. Adjusting the pre-brake (předbrzda).**
> **IMPORTANT:** The pre-brake is adjusted with the **screw and the wing nut (šroub a křídlatá matice)**. When set correctly, the pre-brake ensures the steel rope unwinds from the drum **evenly**. It is set correctly when the wire rope can **still be unwound without any effort**.

**4. Do not use the brake to skid the load.**
> 🛑 **DANGER:** **IT IS NOT APPROPRIATE TO USE THE BRAKE FOR SKIDDING/DRAGGING THE LOAD (vláčení zátěže), because this could damage the rope.** For skidding, use the dedicated **chains (řetězy)**, which are then clipped into the **slots/grooves on the winch cover (drážky na krytu navijáku)**. If you wish to lower/pay out the load, the brake must be released **briefly and gradually, without jerky movements**, because otherwise the drum could turn too fast, overloading and damaging the rope.
