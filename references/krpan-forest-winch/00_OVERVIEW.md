# KRPAN® 4 EP / 5, 6, 8 ERP — Single-Drum Forest Winch (PTO Implement)

> **Source:** KRPAN® 4 EP / 5, 6, 8 ERP Single-Drum Forest Winch — Operating Manual (*Návod k obsluze / "Návod na použití a bezpečnou práci"*), **KRPAN d.o.o.** (Slovenia), revision 03.02.2025. **Translated from the Czech edition** into English; original Czech terms are kept in parentheses on first use. 17 chapters → 8 references.

This is the reference hub for the **KRPAN single-drum forest winch** (*jednobubnový lesní mechanický naviják*) — a mechanical cable winch for skidding/extracting timber. It is an **implement** that mounts on the tractor's **three-point linkage** and is driven by the **PTO at 540 rpm** (it is **not** a front-loader attachment). The tractor (base machine) must supply adequate PTO power, weight and stability.

> 🛑 **DANGER — this is safety-critical equipment.** The wire rope works under high tension; a failed rope or chain can whip/recoil with lethal force. Keep clear of the rope line and the danger zone, anchor/chock the tractor, and never let anyone stand under or near a load. Operate only after reading the full safety chapter.

> ⚠️ **Translation note:** The English here is a faithful translation of the Czech manual. For legally/safety-binding wording (warnings, warranty, CE Declaration of Conformity) defer to the original manual or an official Krpan English edition.

## What it is

- A **PTO-driven, single-drum mechanical forest winch** for skidding logs; four models by size/pull force.
- Attaches to the tractor **three-point linkage** (hitch category by model) and runs off the **PTO at 540 rpm**.
- Controlled by two pull cords from the cab: **grey cord = engage pulling (clutch)**, **red cord = release the brake** to pay out rope.
- Standard features: **proportional brake**, **lower guide pulley**; larger models add options (upper STOP-switch sheave, tool/fuel-tank holders, winch extension).

## The four most important instructions

1. **Rope:** fully unwind then firmly rewind onto the drum; **always keep at least three turns on the drum**; never make loops; **never grease the rope**.
2. **Drive chains (Gall chains):** keep tensioned; lubricate at least weekly or every **50 working hours** with a chain grease gun (drive running, via the grease plugs) — oil must **not** reach the clutch lining.
3. **Pre-brake (předbrzda):** adjust with the screw + wing nut so the rope unwinds **evenly** yet still **without effort**.
4. **Brake:** **do not use the brake to skid the load** (use the dedicated chains in the cover slots); release it **briefly and gradually, no jerks**.

## Quick safety essentials

- Max permitted **pulling angle 30°**; exceeding it risks tractor tip-over. Position the tractor in line with the pull.
- Operator must be **over 18**; work as **two people** (operator + helper) using hand signals. **Not for lifting loads.**
- Anchor/chock the tractor; nobody between tractor and winch unless braked/chocked; keep bystanders out of the danger zone.
- **Annual specialist inspection** required; an incorrectly-shortened PTO cardan shaft voids the warranty.
