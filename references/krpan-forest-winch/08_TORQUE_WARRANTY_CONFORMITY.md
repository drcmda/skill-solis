## 17. Warranty

*(ZÁRUKA — p.43)*

The warranty is valid only if all scheduled service inspections and the prescribed maintenance are carried out by an authorized KRPAN service, in accordance with the operating manual, at the specified time intervals or after the specified number of operating hours. Authorized service points can be found on the manufacturer's website **https://www.vitli-krpan.com/cz/**.

The warranty period is **36 months** from the date of sale, i.e. from the date the goods are handed over to the consumer, subject to exceptions.

### Special exceptions — covered under a 24-month warranty (not 36 months)

- **Hydraulic components:** hydraulic motors, pumps, hoses, seals, valves, oil cooler, hydraulic drive, rotator, low-pressure and mechanical control systems.
- **Electrical components:** sensors, relays, electrical wiring, fans, switches, control lever (joystick), lights, remote-controlled control systems, controllers.
- **Bearings.**
- **Running belts / tracks (rubber).**

> **IMPORTANT:** If the machine is **rented out / hired**, the warranty terms are limited to **24 months**.

### Claims that will be rejected

Claims to remedy defects that are a consequence of the following will be **rejected**:

- negligence;
- any foreign object in or on the damaged machine;
- failure to observe service intervals, or unsuitable / unprofessional service;
- overheating or freezing.

> **IMPORTANT:** The warranty does **not** cover defects, damage, and faults caused by improper and incorrect handling. Modernizations, modifications, and interventions to the machine that were **not** performed and recorded by **PIŠEK – Vitli KRPAN** or by an authorized service **void the machine's warranty**.

### Not covered (consumables / wear service materials)

The warranty also does **not** cover consumable service materials, such as: clutches (spojky), brake linings (brzdová obložení), oils, lubricants, filters, chains (řetězy), guide bar (vodící meč), belts (řemeny), saw blade (pilový list), cutting edges (ostří), …

- The warranty covers repair or free replacement of original defective parts during the warranty period.
- After the warranty period expires, the manufacturer provides repairs, replacement spare parts, and transport for a further **7 years**. The manufacturer is at the same time the authorized service.
- The warranty does not exclude the consumer's rights arising from the seller's liability for defects in the goods.
- The manufacturer guarantees that defects or shortcomings of the goods will, at the buyer's request, be remedied within **30 days**, with the possibility of an extension of a further **15 days** from the date the request to remedy the defect is received. Otherwise, at the buyer's request, the goods will be replaced with new ones. The manufacturer undertakes to extend the warranty period by the duration of the repair.
- **The warranty card is valid only together with the invoice!**
- The warranty statement is valid only within the territory of the **Czech Republic**.

- If, for damage to the equipment or a defect in the material used, it cannot be clearly proven that it arose through the fault of the manufacturing plant, the manufacturer decides on the validity of the warranty claim on the basis of examining the product.
- The warranty does not cover damage caused by unauthorized persons or objects as a result of unprofessional handling, negligence, or failure to observe the instructions stated in the operating manual from the supplier.
- The warranty does not cover damage that occurred during transport, or damage caused by excessive operation or use of the machine.
- The warranty also does not cover parts whose damage occurred as a result of excessive wear.
- The warranty **becomes void** if the equipment was repaired or even damaged by an unauthorized service organization or persons.
- After a damage event has concluded, free compensation for it or an extension of the warranty period is excluded.
- If the equipment needs to be repaired, hand the **warranty card and the original invoice** to the authorized person. If the equipment or a part of it is sent for repair, send the above-mentioned documents at the same time.
- Without the written consent of the manufacturer, no one is authorized to change the data in the warranty card or to grant any oral or written permissions. No direct or indirect personal or material compensation will be provided for damages arising from the fact that the equipment was out of operation.
