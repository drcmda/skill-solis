# 02 — Warning Signs & Safe Working

> **Source:** KRPAN® 4 EP / 5, 6, 8 ERP Single-Drum Forest Winch — Operating Manual (Návod k obsluze / "Návod na použití a bezpečnou práci"), KRPAN d.o.o. (Slovenia), revision 03.02.2025 — translated from the Czech edition — pp.9–14.

This chapter reproduces the machine's warning and information decals, the at-a-glance quick instructions, and the full set of safe-working rules for the single-drum mechanical forest winch (jednobubnový lesní mechanický naviják). The winch attaches to the tractor's **three-point linkage (tříbodové připojení)** and is driven from the tractor **PTO / cardan shaft (kardanová / kloubová hřídel) at a maximum of 540 rpm**; the wire rope runs under very high tension, so the danger-zone, rope-line, and anchoring rules below are safety-critical.

> 🛑 **DANGER:** Failure to observe the safety and warning decals can lead to serious injury to the machine and to persons in its vicinity!
> [lit.: "Nedodržení bezpečnostních a výstražných štítků může vést k vážnému zranění stroje a osob v jeho blízkosti!"]

### 3.1 Quick Instructions (Rychlé pokyny) — KRPAN® 4 EP / 5, 6, 8 ERP

> **NOTE:** The at-a-glance quick-instruction decal carries eight numbered panels (1–8). Each panel and its full instruction are reproduced below. Each item points to a detailed chapter that **must** be read before that operation.

1. **Marked lifting point for transporting the forest winch.** The machine may be transported **only** by crane or forklift and using approved lifting equipment. Transport is permitted **only** when the winch is in the transport position and the pin (čep) is secured by its lock! Before transport you must read and follow chapter **"13. Transport"** in this manual.

2. **Marked PTO / cardan drive-shaft connection (přípojný kloubový hřídel).** Operating speeds: **minimum required 220 rpm, maximum permitted 540 rpm, ideal 280 rpm.**

3. **Marked screw for adjusting the pre-brake (předbrzda).** Turning the screw **right increases** the pre-brake force; turning it **left decreases** it. The pre-brake is factory-set, but each user may adapt the braking force! Before adjusting the pre-brake you must read and follow chapter **"6.2 Pre-brake (Předbrzda)"**.

4. **Tensioning the drive chains (hnací řetězy):** carry out the first time after **15 operating hours**, then **every 100 operating hours**. Use a **19 mm open-end wrench** for adjustment. Before adjusting you must read and follow chapter **"8.1 Tensioning the drive chain (Napínání hnacího řetězu)"**.

5. **Engaging the pulling (winching) function.** Pulling the **grey rope (šedé lano)** in the direction of the arrow on the quick-instruction decal activates the pulling function. Before starting work with the forest winch you must read and follow this operating- and safe-working manual!

6. **Releasing the automatic winch brake.** After pulling ends, the **automatic differential brake (automatická diferenciální brzda)** holds the load. To release tension in the steel cable (ocelové lano) you must pull the **red rope (červené lano)** in the direction of the arrow. After the brake is released, the steel cable can be unwound from the drum (buben). Before starting work you must read and follow this operating- and safe-working manual!

7. **Folding the upper protective grille (horní ochranná mříž) down:** loosen all three marked bolted joints. When refitting the grille to the upper position, ensure all three joints are properly tightened. Observe the table of maximum tightening torques for bolts and nuts in **chapter 16**.

8. **Marked lubrication points on the forest winch.** The left figure shows the **grease** lubrication points; the right figure shows the **central lubrication system for spray lubrication**. Before lubricating, read and follow chapter **"8. Maintenance (Údržba)"** and all its sub-sections!

## 4. Instructions for Safe Work (Návod pro bezpečnou práci)

> 🛑 **DANGER:** When working with the winch you must give the highest priority to safety! To prevent accidents, please read and follow these instructions carefully.
> [lit.: "Při práci s navijákem musíte věnovat bezpečnosti největší pozornost!"]

1. In addition to the instructions in this operating manual, observe **all** generally applicable safety regulations!
2. The winch must be operated safely and in accordance with occupational-safety regulations!
3. The winch may be used **only** by persons familiar with its operation, with the hazards of winching, and with this manual!
4. Operating / working with the winch is permitted **only to persons over 18 years of age!**
5. The warning and safety decals on the machine give important instructions for safe operation. Observe them for your own safety!
6. When using public roads, observe traffic signs and signals!
7. **Only PTO / cardan shafts (kloubové hřídele) of the correct length may be used; if an incorrect length is used the warranty will not be honoured!**
8. During work it is mandatory to use personal protective equipment (helmet, gloves, suitable footwear, and carry a first-aid kit…)!
9. The operator's clothing must fit close to the body. Avoid loose clothing!
10. Before driving and connecting, check the immediate surroundings (children)! Ensure sufficient visibility!
11. Riding on the winch during transport is not permitted!
12. The winch must be connected according to the instructions!
13. For road travel, prepare the machine to the prescribed condition!
14. Always adapt travel speed to the surrounding conditions! When driving up, down, or across a slope, avoid sudden turning!

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** No one may stay between the tractor and the winch unless the tractor is secured against movement by the brake or by chocking with a wedge (podložení klínem)! (Rule 15)

16. Until the winch components have come to rest, it is not permitted to touch it!
17. Regularly check the fastening / mounting of the winch!
18. Before use, the winch must be inspected visually. **At least once a year** it must be checked by a specialist!

> 🛑 **DANGER:** The winch must not be used for other purposes, e.g. **lifting loads (Figure 6)!** (Rule 19)

20. **At every intervention on the winch, the cardan / PTO shaft must be disengaged — i.e. the tractor engine must unconditionally be switched off!**
21. If welding on the forest winch may be required, the control console must be disconnected!
22. **The safety components of the winch must not be removed!**
23. For the tow rope, use a rope of the corresponding strength and quality!
24. **A damaged rope must be replaced immediately!**

> **IMPORTANT:** Use a rope of such length that, when fully wound onto the drum, a clearance of **2 rope diameters** remains to the outer diameter of the drum. When the rope is fully unwound, **at least three turns (náviny)** of the tow rope must remain on the drum! (Rule 25)

26. The helper must not attach a load to the winch until the tractor operator has been informed!

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** The winch is operated only from a safe position where there is no danger from the load, the rope, or standing trees. The tractor operator's seat also counts as a safe position (if using a forestry tractor). The winch has a safety net (bezpečnostní síť) installed which, per these instructions, must not be removed! (Rule 27)

> 🛑 **DANGER:** It is especially dangerous to stand in front of the tree that is to be pulled — **Figure 2!** (Rule 28)

> 🛑 **DANGER:** When using a snatch / relay pulley (relé řemenice), watch out for the **danger triangle** — the hazardous area in which it is not permitted to stay during pulling (Figure 3)! (Rule 29)

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** When pulling, observe the **maximum permitted angle of 30 degrees (Figure 4)!** (Rule 30)

> 🛑 **DANGER:** On dangerous terrain, or if the maximum permitted pulling angle is not observed, there is a risk of **tip-over / roll-over (Figure 5)!** (Rule 31)

32. **Do not stay in the danger zone (Figures 2, 3, 4, 5, 6)!**

> **IMPORTANT:** Work with the winch must be carried out correctly by **two workers** (the tractor operator and a helper), who must communicate with each other by **hand signals!** (Rule 33)
> Hand signals on the decal: **rope pulling-in (Vlečení lana)**, **STOP**, and **rope paying-out (Popouštění lana)**.

34. The person operating the winch must continuously watch the load during pulling. If the terrain does not allow this, a helper should assist!
35. The tractor to which the winch is attached must have a **minimum tyre tread that still complies with road regulations!** Otherwise, **chains must be fitted to the wheels! Chains are also mandatory when working on snow and ice!**
36. When unhitching, first choose a suitably firm and level surface. Secure the winch using the support leg (podpěrná noha)!
37. In the area of the three-point linkage bars there is a risk of injury from pinching and crushing (zmáčknutí a stlačení)!
38. Before any work on the winch, disconnect the winch from the tractor and read about winch maintenance!

> 🛑 **DANGER:** Always ensure during work that the forestry chain is correctly guided over the end fitting / terminal (koncovka) (Figure 7)! (Rule 39)
> **IF THE CHAIN AND END FITTING ARE USED INCORRECTLY, THE CHAIN OR END FITTING MAY DEFORM, WHICH CAN HAVE FATAL CONSEQUENCES!**
> **ONLY BY FOLLOWING THE INSTRUCTIONS WILL SAFE WORK BE SUCCESSFUL!**
> [lit.: "PŘI NESPRÁVNÉM POUŽITÍ ŘETĚZU A KONCOVKY MŮŽE DOJÍT K DEFORMACI ŘETĚZU NEBO KONCOVKY, COŽ MŮŽE MÍT FATÁLNÍ NÁSLEDKY! POUZE PŘI DODRŽOVÁNÍ INSTRUKCÍ BUDE BEZPEČNÁ PRÁCE ÚSPĚŠNÁ!"]

### 4.1 Operation with PTO / Cardan Connection (Provoz s kardanovým připojením)

1. **You may use only PTO / cardan shafts of the length specified by the manufacturer!**
2. On the cardan connection, the tubes (trubice), joint guards (kryty kloubů) and connection guards (kryty připojení) must be present and in flawless condition!
3. Pay attention to the prescribed guards on the cardan shaft, both during transport and during work!
4. Connect and disconnect the cardan shaft **only when the cardan coupling is disengaged.** At the same time you must switch off the tractor and remove the key!
5. Always ensure correct mounting and guarding of the cardan shaft!
6. Secure the cardan-shaft guard against rotation with the retaining chain / restraint rope (závěsné lano)!
7. Before engaging the cardan connection, make sure the selected rotational speed and direction match the permitted speed and direction of the winch! (max **540 rpm**, correct direction)
8. Before engaging and during operation of the cardan connection, make sure that no one is staying in the danger zone of the winch!

> 🛑 **DANGER:** Never connect the cardan/PTO coupling while the engine is running! (Rule 9)

### 4.2 Adjusting / Shortening the PTO Cardan Shaft (Přizpůsobení kardanové hřídele)

The length of the cardan shaft must be adapted to the particular tractor. Determine the exact length as follows:

1. Connect the winch to the tractor.
2. Remove the cardan shaft separately and connect the individual shaft halves to the tractor and to the machine. Then compare them against each other.
3. Check whether, at maximum raising and lowering of the three-point linkage bars, the cardan tubes still overlap by **min. 200 mm**, and that in the horizontal position the cardan shaft does not bottom out / hit the block (the tube overlap must still tolerate **min. 40 mm**). **(F1)**
4. If the tubes need shortening, shorten **both** the cardan (drive) tube and the safety/guard tube by the **same length. (F2, F3)**
5. Deburr the ends of the individual tubes, remove the filings, and grease the mating surfaces well. **(F4)**
6. Attach the cardan shaft with the retaining/restraint rope (závěsné lano). **(G1)**
7. When you stop using the machine: set the tractor throttle to minimum and switch off the PTO (vývodový hřídel), disconnect the chain, and remove the PTO shaft from the tractor. Then secure the cardan shaft on its hanger on the machine, or store it separately in another (designated) place. **(G2)**

> Figure 8 illustrates steps F1–F4 (overlap check and tube shortening) and G1–G2 (attachment and storage).

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** Only PTO / cardan shafts of the correct length may be used for work; **if an incorrect length is used, the warranty will not be honoured!**
> [lit.: "Pro práci lze použít pouze kloubové hřídele správné délky, v případě použití nesprávné délky nebude záruka uznána!"]
