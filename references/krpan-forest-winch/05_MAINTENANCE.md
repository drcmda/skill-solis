# 05 — Winch Maintenance

> **Source:** KRPAN® 4 EP / 5, 6, 8 ERP Single-Drum Forest Winch — Operating Manual (Návod k obsluze / "Návod na použití a bezpečnou práci"), KRPAN d.o.o. (Slovenia), revision 03.02.2025 — translated from the Czech edition — pp.29–33.

This chapter (manual Ch. 8, *Údržba navijáku*) covers routine maintenance of the single-drum mechanical forest winch: tensioning and lubricating the drive chains, lubricating the articulated/joint shaft and the PTO cardan shaft (kardanová hřídel), and the overall maintenance schedule. The winch mounts on the tractor's three-point linkage and is driven by the PTO at 540 rpm; all maintenance is performed with the machine attached to the base machine.

> 🛑 **DANGER:** Before starting any maintenance, **switch off the tractor, remove the key, and wait until all moving parts have come to a complete stop!** (Exception: drive-chain spray lubrication via the central lubrication system — see §05.2 — which is deliberately done with the PTO shaft running.)

## 05.1 — Tensioning the Drive Chain (Napínání pohonného řetězu)

During operation the drive chain (pohonný řetěz) stretches somewhat under load, so it must be checked and re-adjusted several times to prevent excessive wear of the entire chain drive. The chain must **not** be over-tightened — it must retain **1–3 mm of free play (vůle)**.

| Item | Specification |
|---|---|
| Permissible chain free play (slack) | **1–3 mm** |
| First tensioning | After approx. **15 operating hours** |
| Subsequent checks | Every **100 operating hours** |

### Tensioning procedure

1. Unscrew the bolts on the **front protective cover (přední ochranný kryt)** and remove it.
2. Then partially loosen the bolts on the **drive cover (kryt pohonu)**.
3. Unscrew the nut (matice).
4. Begin by tensioning the **main chain (hlavní řetěz)**: tighten the bolt while checking the chain tension by hand. The chain must allow only minimal oscillation / movement (minimální kmitání).
5. Once tensioning is complete, tighten the **lock nut (pojistná matice)**.
6. Chain 2 (řetěz 2) is tensioned by means of the bolt on the drive. First unscrew the nut, then turn out the chain-tensioning bolt.
7. Once the desired chain tension is reached, tighten the nut of the tensioning bolt and re-tighten the drive bolts (hnací šrouby).

**Figure 13 (Obrázek 13) — drive-cover tensioning points, as labelled on the machine:**

- Chain 2 (řetěz 2) tensioning system (napínací systém řetězu 2) — at the rear/drive end.
- Front main-chain (hlavní řetěz) tensioning system — at the upper front of the drive housing.
- 1 — Loosen the drive bolts (hnací šrouby) before tensioning.
- 2 — Adjusting bolt (seřizovací šroub).
- 3 — Lock nut (pojistná matice) on the main-chain tensioner.
- 4 — Adjusting nut (seřizovací matice).
- 5 — Pro-tensioning bolt (napínací šroub) for the main chain.

> **NOTE:** Drive chains are wear parts (rychle se opotřebí) and are therefore **NOT covered by the warranty!**

## 05.2 — Drive-Chain Maintenance / Lubrication (Údržba hnacího řetězu)

Users who run the drive chains (hnací řetěz) under heavy-duty operation are advised to lubricate them **at least once a week, or at least every 50 working hours.**

Lubrication procedure:

- Lubricate using **KRPAN chain spray lubricant (mazivo na řetězy ve spreji KRPAN)** applied through the **grease plugs / lubrication nipples (mazací zátky)**.
- Before lubricating, remove the protective caps (ochranné krytky) on the plugs.
- It is also recommended to check the chain tension at the same time.
- After lubricating, refit the caps onto the plugs ("svíčky") so that no dirt or foreign matter can get in.

**Figure 14 (Obrázek 14) — labelled detail:**

- **Central lubrication system (centrální mazací systém)** — the spray manifold.
- "Lubricate the drive chain with KRPAN spray. Place the plug back onto the nipple (zátka) after lubricating."
- "Before lubricating, remove the protective cap (krytka) on the nipple, and refit it once lubrication is complete."
- The drive chains (hnací řetězy) are lubricated by spray fed through the central lubrication system.

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** Spray lubrication of the drive chains via the central lubrication system **MUST be carried out with the PTO output shaft (vývodový hřídel) running**, and **ONLY KRPAN spray may be used for lubrication!**

> **IMPORTANT:** If you find traces of lubricant on the forest winch, it may be caused by **over-lubrication of the drive chains with spray!**

## 05.3 — Lubricating the Articulated/Joint Shaft (Mazání kloubového hřídele)

Lubricate the output / joint shaft (vývodový / kloubový hřídel) as needed — for example, if a problem occurs when connecting the PTO shaft to the machine (the shaft slides with difficulty over the mating shaft, i.e. the telescoping halves are stiff).

| Item | Specification |
|---|---|
| Recommended lubricant | Plastic grease — **lithium (lithium)**, or a spray with similar properties |
| When to lubricate | **Only with the machine switched off** — never during operation |

**Figure 15 (Obrázek 15) — labelled detail:**

- "Lubricate the joint shaft (kloubový hřídel) — apply lithium grease or a spray with similar properties."
- "Lubricate the joint shaft only with the machine switched off; in no case lubricate the joint shaft during operation."

> 🛑 **DANGER:** Lubricate the joint shaft **ONLY with the machine switched off. In no case lubricate the joint shaft while it is running!**

## 05.4 — PTO Cardan-Shaft Maintenance (Údržba kardanové hřídele)

To drive the winch you must use a PTO cardan shaft (kardanová hřídel) of appropriate quality and strength. The recommended cardan shaft is the **WALTERSCHEID W 2400 SD25**, catalogue no. **20000337**, which is made specifically for this type of winch, or a cardan shaft from another manufacturer with comparable properties.

### 05.4.1 — Cardan-Shaft Lubrication

| Item | Specification |
|---|---|
| Grease type | **Lithium grease (lithiové mazivo)** |
| Consistency / NLGI class | **NLGI 2 (NL-GI2)** |
| Max. grease quantity per lubrication point | **15 g = 5 strokes / presses (stlačení)** of the grease gun |

**Lubrication points:**

- **Crosses / universal-joint spiders (kříže) (1) and protective bearings (ochranná ložiska) (2):**
  Push the protective bearing back, lubricate both the cross (kříž) and the protective bearing, then return them to their original position.
- **Telescoping tubes (trubky) (3):**
  Pull the cardan shaft apart; at the midpoint, remove the protective cover from the inner tube (vnitřní trubka) and lubricate the inner tube.

**Figure 16 (Obrázek 16):** Cardan-shaft assembly showing the lettered lubrication points — the two end yokes with crosses/protective bearings (points 1 and 2) and the telescoping tube section (point 3).

**Figure 17 (Obrázek 17) — grease quantity per point, as marked on the diagram:**

| Lubrication point along the shaft | Grease quantity |
|---|---|
| Joint / cross (end 1) | **15 g** |
| Telescoping tube section | **6 g** |
| Telescoping tube section | **6 g** |
| Joint / cross (end 2) | **15 g** |

### 05.4.2 — Lubrication Interval

Careful handling increases the reliability and service life of the cardan shaft.

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** Using the cardan shaft **without its guard, or with a damaged guard**, or with the guard-retaining chain incorrectly positioned (where one is required), is **prohibited.**

> ⚠️ **WARNING:** Before starting work, the position and proper function of **all guard components must be verified.**

> **IMPORTANT:** Replace damaged or missing parts **only with original parts.**

> **IMPORTANT:** Any modification other than those described in the manual is **prohibited.**

> 🛑 **DANGER:** Pay special attention to **regular lubrication of the cross (kříž) and the cardan tubes every 8 hours.** Otherwise they may be damaged, leading to subsequent damage to the cardan shaft and to the winch drive. The protective tubes and funnels/cones (trychtýře) must also be in flawless condition.

Lubricate the cardan shaft according to the manufacturer's instructions (Figure 18 / Obrázek 18).

> **NOTE:** Where this winch's chart value and the §05.4.2 text differ, the binding requirement remains the **8-hour** interval for the crosses and cardan tubes.

## 05.5 — Maintenance Plan / Schedule (Plán údržby)

Perform a **visual inspection and function test of the winch before every start of work.** Check the following:

- That all bolts and nuts are tightened.
- That there is no mechanical damage on the winch.
- That all pin locks/retainers (pojistky čepů) at the winch attachment points are set/secured.
- That the shaft is connected and the **safety chain/cord (lanko) of the cardan shaft guard is attached.**
- That the tractor's lower links (spodní páky traktoru) are properly fixed, to prevent horizontal shifting of the winch.
- That the clutch (spojka) functions correctly.
- That the rope-pull-out force (síla vyvlékání lana) is correctly set.

> 🛑 **DANGER:** All defects must be remedied **before starting work!**

### Consolidated maintenance schedule (task vs. interval)

| Task | Interval / Trigger | Material / Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Visual inspection & function test (bolts/nuts, damage, pin locks, shaft & guard cord, tractor lower-link fixing, clutch, rope-pull-out force) | **Before every start of work** | Remedy all defects before working |
| First drive-chain (pohonný řetěz) tensioning | After approx. **15 operating hours** | Maintain 1–3 mm free play |
| Drive-chain tension check | Every **100 operating hours** | Maintain 1–3 mm free play; chains are non-warranty wear parts |
| Drive-chain (hnací řetěz) lubrication | **Weekly**, or every **50 working hours** (heavy-duty use) | KRPAN chain **spray only**, via grease plugs; done with PTO **running** through the central lubrication system; refit caps afterward |
| Articulated/joint shaft (kloubový hřídel) lubrication | **As needed** (e.g. stiff telescoping when connecting) | Lithium grease or similar spray; **machine off only** |
| Cardan-shaft crosses (kříže) & tubes (trubky) lubrication | Every **8 operating hours** | Lithium grease, **NLGI 2**; max **15 g (5 strokes)** per point (15 g at each cross, 6 g per tube section) |

> **IMPORTANT:** Drive chains are wear parts and are not covered by the warranty. Use only KRPAN spray on the drive chains and only original spare parts on the cardan shaft.
