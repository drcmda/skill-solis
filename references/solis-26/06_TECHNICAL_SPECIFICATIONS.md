## Quick-Reference Highlights

- **Engine:** Mitsubishi MVS3L2 — 3-cyl, 4-stroke, naturally aspirated, water-cooled, OHV; 1318 cc; bore × stroke 78 × 92 mm; compression 22:1; firing order 1-3-2.
- **Rated speed:** 2700 rpm (Stage III) / 2500 rpm (Stage V). Low idle 1000 ± 30 / ± 50 rpm.
- **Valve clearance:** 0.25 mm intake / 0.25 mm exhaust.
- **Transmission:** Constant-mesh synchromesh, single mechanical clutch; 9 forward + 9 reverse (9+9).
- **Hydraulic lift capacity:** 600 kg (1322.8 lbs).
- **Brakes:** Oil-immersed. Turning circle min. diameter 5.14 m (LHS) / 5.09 m (RHS).
- **Steering:** Power steering.
- **PTO:** Type-I, 540 rpm @ 2565 ERPM (also 540 @ 2083 ERPM).
- **Tyres:** Front 6.00 × 12; Rear 8.30 × 20.
- **Dimensions:** Wheelbase 1570 mm; length 2945 mm; width 1245 mm; height (ROPS) 2442 mm; ground clearance 240 mm; front track 880–990 mm; rear track 920–970 mm.
- **Weight (with driver):** 1120 kg total (Front 425 kg / Rear 580 kg).
- **Implement RPM guidance:** Rotavator & Sprayer 2000–2100; Cultivator & Disc Harrow 2100–2500; Rear Lawn Mover, Flail Mower, Box Blade, Bottom Plough 2000–2300; Rotary Cutter 2000–2100. PTO-driven implements run 540 PTO RPM @ 2565 ERPM.
