## 1.1 Using this Operator Manual

Reading this manual helps the operator and others avoid personal injury or damage to the tractor; the information helps use the tractor in the safest and most effective way.

- This manual is an important part of the tractor and should be kept **with the tractor even when you sell it**.
- Always specify the tractor **Chassis** and **Engine** serial numbers when ordering replacement parts — this facilitates correct and faster delivery of required parts from the dealer. Keep your own record of these numbers for quick reference when ordering parts.

> **NOTE (Attachments):** If you have an attachment, use the safety and operating information in the attachment operator's manual together with the tractor operator's manual to operate the attachment safely and correctly.

> **NOTE (Languages):** This manual and the safety signs on your tractor may also be available in various languages — contact your dealer for more information.

> **NOTE (Illustrations):** The tractor shown in this manual may differ slightly from your tractor, but will be similar enough to help you understand the instructions.

**Directional terminology convention** — used throughout the manual to avoid confusion:

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Left Side (LH) | Left side of the tractor when **facing in the direction of forward travel** |
| Right Side (RH) | Right side of the tractor when facing in the direction of forward travel |
| Front Side | The **radiator end** of the tractor |
| Rear Side | The **draw bar end** of the tractor |

A top-view tractor diagram illustrates this convention, labelling the four sides around the tractor outline: **Front** (radiator end, pointing left in the figure), **Rear** (draw bar end), **Right** and **Left**.

## 1.2 Chassis Serial Number

**Reference: Fig. A — Chassis Serial Number.**

- The chassis number is **punched on the right side of the front axle bracket** of the tractor (see Fig. A).
- If the number is difficult to read, it can also be found on the **Statutory Plate** (see §1.4).

---

## 1.3 Engine Serial Number

**Reference: Fig. B — Engine Serial Number (shown two ways: Punch and Sticker).**

- The engine serial number is **stamped on the upper side of the Fuel Injection Pump (FIP) installation part, located on the right side of the cylinder block**.
- For easy reference, the engine serial number is **also mentioned on the valve (rocker) cover of the engine** (see Fig. B).

---

## 1.4 Statutory Plate

**Reference: Fig. C — Statutory Plate (shown as C1 & C2).**

- The chassis number is **also engraved on the Statutory Plate**.
- The Statutory Plate is located on the **left-hand side fender** (Fig. C1 & C2).

The Statutory Plate (Fig. C) is a black plate headed **"SONALIKA / INTERNATIONAL TRACTORS LTD."** with two mounting holes. Below the header it carries (top to bottom):

- Three blank engraved data lines (for model / type / serial identification).
- A permissible-mass block, in **kg**:
  - Total permissible mass: `______ kg`
  - **A-1:** `______ kg` (permissible front-axle load)
  - **A-2:** `______ kg` (permissible rear-axle load)
- A towable-load / tyre matrix table with column headers **T-1, T-2, T-3 (and further T-columns)** against row headers **B-1, B-2, B-3, B-4** — used to record permissible drawbar/towed loads per configuration.

## 1.5 ROPS Certificate Plate

**Reference: Fig. D — ROPS Certificate Plate (EEC). — Optional.**

- The ROPS certificate plate is **riveted on the ROPS** (Roll-Over Protective Structure / safety frame).
- Information about the **ROPS serial number** and **tractor model** is engraved on the ROPS plate.
- For countries under the **EEC**, the ROPS Certificate plate is used as shown in Fig. D.

The EEC ROPS plate (Fig. D) carries the **Sonalika "INTERNATIONAL" logo** and the maker name **"INTERNATIONAL TRACTORS LIMITED"**, four corner rivet holes, and the following:

- An EEC type-approval mark box showing **"e₅"** with type-approval number **"00045 U5S"** below it.
- **ROPS Serial No.** `______` (engraved field).
- **Tractor Model** `______` (engraved field).

> **NOTE:** The PDF text layer for this plate also renders the template fields **"V1"**, **"INTERNATIONAL TRACTORS LIMITED"**, **"TRACTOR TYPE T 2"**, **"ROPS Serial No."**, **"Tractor Model"**, together with approval characters **e11**, **3056**, **S** — these correspond to the older / alternate plate text. The plate image as printed shows the **e₅ / 00045 U5S** approval mark.

## 1.6 Universal Symbols

As a guide to the operation of the tractor, various universal (ISO-style) symbols are used on the instruments, controls and other places on the tractor. The symbols and their meanings are reproduced below. Icon descriptions are provided so each pictogram can be recognised on the machine.

| Symbol (pictogram described) | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Open book / manual | **Read Operator's Manual** |
| Triangle with exclamation mark | **Safety Alert Symbol** |
| Fuel pump | **Fuel Level** |
| Tachometer / "rpm" dial | **Engine Rotational Speed** |
| "(P)" — circle with P, brake disc | **Parking Brake** |
| Filter/element with airflow arrow | **Air Cleaner Clogging Sensor** |
| Battery with +/– terminals | **Battery Charging Condition** |
| Oil-can drip | **Engine Oil-Pressure** |
| Flashing arrows (left/right) | **Turn Signal** |
| PTO shaft with circle, "off" | **Power Take-Off Clutch Control — Off Position** |
| PTO shaft with circle, "on" | **Power Take-Off Clutch Control — On Position** |
| Double red triangles (hazard) | **Hazard Warning Lights** |
| Headlamp with light rays | **Master Lighting Switch** |
| Headlamp with downward rays | **Headlight — Low Beam** |
| Headlamp with horizontal rays | **Headlight — High Beam** |
| Horn / loudspeaker | **Audible Warning Device** |
| Tractor outline, all 4 wheels driven | **Four-Wheel Drive — On** |
| Tractor outline, rear wheels driven | **Four-Wheel Drive — Off** |
| Thermometer in liquid | **Engine Coolant — Temperature** |
| Small rabbit/hare | **Fast** |
| Tortoise / snail | **Slow** |
| "P" with check / brake | **Parking Brake Indication** |
| "540" with PTO shaft | **PTO 540** |
| "540E" with PTO shaft | **PTO 540 Economy** |
| Axle with locked differential | **Differential Lock** |
| Implement lowered (arrow down) | **Hydraulic Control — Lowered Position** |
| Implement raised (arrow up) | **Hydraulic Control — Raised Position** |
| Engine with rpm/throttle | **Engine Speed Control** |
| Cylinder with inward arrow | **Remote Cylinder — Retract** |
| Cylinder with outward arrow | **Remote Cylinder — Extend** |
| Engine outline with stop | **Engine Stop** |

> **NOTE:** "540" and "540E" denote the standard 540 rpm PTO and the 540 Economy (540E) PTO mode respectively.
